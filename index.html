Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is there a difference between dentists and orthodontists?

Dentists deal with a wide variety of oral health problems. Orthodontists are dentists who specialise in the alignment of the teeth and jaws.

2. Can dentists treat orthodontic conditions?

Yes, a few dentists are qualified as both orthodontists and dentists. You can check with Divyakarunyam Speciality Dental Clinic about this information.

3. How often should I visit a dentist?

It is often recommended that you visit a dentist twice a year or once every three to six months for routine cleaning and checking to avoid complex surgeries.
